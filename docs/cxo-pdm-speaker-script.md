# CXO Speaker Script (30-40s per slide)

## Slide 1 - Executive Opening
"Thank you for the time. This solution combines predictive and prescriptive maintenance to directly protect EBITDA. Instead of isolated technical alerts, we provide a single decision surface where operations and finance can see risk, cost, and action priority together. This is designed for executive visibility and frontline execution."

## Slide 2 - Why Predictive + Prescriptive
"Predictive analytics tells us what is likely to fail and when. Prescriptive analytics tells us what to do next, in business terms. Without prescriptive logic, teams face alert fatigue and delayed action. With both, we prioritize interventions by value-at-risk, not by noise."

## Slide 3 - Bottom-Line Impact
"Every recommendation is tied to financial consequence: avoided loss versus intervention cost. That means maintenance decisions are no longer a cost-center debate; they become value-protection decisions. This is how we move from reactive firefighting to financially optimized operations."

## Slide 4 - End-to-End Databricks Architecture
"This is the full architecture from OT signals to business outcomes. OT sources land in Delta, are transformed through governed DLT pipelines, scored by MLflow-managed models, and surfaced in app workflows with AI assistance. The same architecture embeds governance, lineage, auditability, and localization."

## Slide 5 - Governance and Trust
"For CXO adoption, trust is non-negotiable. Unity Catalog governance, data lineage, model versioning, and action-level audit trails ensure each recommendation is explainable and controllable. Human operators remain in control through approve, defer, and reject workflows."

## Slide 6 - 5-Minute Live Demo Story
"In a live demo, we open Geo Intelligence, select a high-risk asset, review anomaly and RUL signals, then show the financial tradeoff in business currency. We use AI to validate root cause and action sequence, and finally capture an operator decision. The key message is speed-to-decision with business clarity."

## Slide 7 - KPI Framework
"Success is measured with hard metrics: unplanned downtime reduction, improved planned intervention ratio, faster decision lead time, and increasing avoided-loss run rate. We track these weekly during pilot so the business case is continuously validated."

## Slide 8 - CXO Decision Ask
"The ask is a focused pilot on one critical site and asset class over 30-45 days. We baseline current performance, quantify value protection, and produce a scale blueprint for additional plants or fabs. This gives fast evidence and a governed path to enterprise rollout."

## Optional 20s Add-On (AE/SA Positioning)
"For customer-facing teams, this is highly reusable across five industries - mining, energy, water, automotive, and semiconductor. AEs and SAs can reuse one end-to-end value story and switch currency/localization to match regional executive audiences."
