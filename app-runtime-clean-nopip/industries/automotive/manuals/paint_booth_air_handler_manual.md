# Paint Booth Air Handler Maintenance Manual (Excerpt)

Equipment Class: Paint shop AHU fan train and filtration bank
Revision: A1.9

## Failure Mode Guidance
- Failure mode: airflow_instability
- Typical indicators: static_pressure variance, fan_vibration increase, VOC extraction underperformance
- Confirmation criteria: static pressure deviation > 12 percent for 30 minutes with fan vibration > 6.5 mm/s

## Inspection and Repair
- Inspection interval: every 120 operating hours
- Torque: 24 N-m on impeller hub fasteners
- Part number: FIL-PAINT-H13
- Part number: BRG-FAN-7310

## Parts and Readiness
- Minimum on-hand recommendation: 4 H13 filter sets per booth line
- Lead time for FIL-PAINT-H13: 10 days
- If humidity excursions coincide with airflow faults, include damper actuator inspection

## Prescriptive Action Notes
- If anomaly score >= 0.72 with static pressure deviation trend, intervene in next shift
- Validate airflow balance after filter or bearing replacement
- Log corrective action outcomes to improve prescriptive optimizer precision
